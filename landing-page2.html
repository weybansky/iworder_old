ABOUT

iWorder is a soon-to-be launched social network for writers, poets and creative word artists across Africa. On iWorder, users can connect with diverse worders across the continent, interact with like minds, share ideas, build their niche and become even better at their craft!

A new hub for African writers to share their art and story with the world, worders on iWorder have the chance to be tutored, critiqued, and appreciated by renowned and award winning African writers across the globe.

iWorder is set to launch on the 1st of March, 2018. Want to beta-test iWorder before its launch, click here.

MEET THE IWORDER TEAM

Abiodun Praise

Abiodun Praise is the founder and leader of the iWorder team. A lover of unique creativity and style, he is most at home when performing his own poetry.

Joseph Akinnawonu

Joseph Akinnawonu is a graduate of English and Literary Studies from Obafemi Awolowo University. He write poetry in the language of ordinariness exploring Afican Identity and social issues

Ayomide Fasan

Ayomy is a writer and editor. Her writings are known for their resonant themes on faith, belief, and christainity. She loves people!

Oluwabukunmi Ajani

Oluwabukunmi Ajani is a young Nigerian who writes because words constantly lie at the tip of her fingers, waiting. She has a notable affection for art and humanities and dreams of a world where there is the space and freedom for uninhibited expression and creativity.

TR Philips

TR Philips is a spoken word poet and a writer. His writings involve mainly songwriting and of course, poetry; Presiding over Poetic People College, Nigeria

Ajani Ololade

Ajani Ololade is a budding Nigerian writer. She is passionate about nature and its elements, some of her poems reflect that.

Olúwatóyìn Fálékè

Olúwatóyìn Fálékè is a Nigerian writer with the unique ability to view life from all its different perspectives. She writes about relatable experiences, and the realities of life with a sense of humour.

S.T. Komolafe

S.T. Komolafe is a poet and an undergraduate of the Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife. He enjoys reading and reviewing literary works.

Abiodun Promise

Promise is a medical student whose love for creative poetry has seen him write himself into words & rhymes healing people's wounds. He has a thing for jarring twists!
