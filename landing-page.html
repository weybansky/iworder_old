About iWorder

iWorder is a burgeoning social network for writers, poets and creative word artists all across Africa. Here, you can connect with diverse worders across the continent, sharing, interacting and building your own niche.

A new hub for African writers to share their art and story with the world, worders on iWorder have the chance to be tutored, critiqued, and appreciated by renowned and award winning writers within the continent.
